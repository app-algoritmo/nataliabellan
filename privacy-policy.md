## 5. Your Rights

Under the LGPD and GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Access** — request a copy of the data we hold about you
- **Rectification** — correct incomplete or inaccurate data
- **Erasure** — request the deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Portability** — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Objection or restriction** — object to or limit certain processing activities
- **Withdrawal of consent** — withdraw consent at any time
- **Unsubscribe** — opt out of communications at any time via the unsubscribe link or by contacting us directly
- **Complaint** — file a complaint with the ANPD (Brazil) or the competent supervisory authority in your country

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [nb@nataliabellan.org](mailto:nb@nataliabellan.org). We will respond within a maximum of 15 business days.
